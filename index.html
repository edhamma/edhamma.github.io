Reference redirection

The edhamma.github.io/xref.html is providing a simple stable redirection service to the HTML format, consuming book (currently one of vism, vimm and mctb2) and ref parameters, so e.g. https://edhamma.github.io/xref.html?book=vism&ref=I.40 will redirect to Visuddhimagga, I.40. This might be useful to insert stable hyperlinks into other texts.
